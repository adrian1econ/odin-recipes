Chicken Tinga Tacos

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) cooked shredded chicken
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1-2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce (from a can)
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Corn tortillas
  • Toppings: avocado, cilantro, lime, queso fresco

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion in oil until soft. Add garlic and cook 1 min.
  2. Blend chipotle peppers and tomatoes into a sauce.
  3. Add sauce and cumin to pan. Simmer 10 mins.
  4. Stir in shredded chicken. Simmer 5–10 more mins.
  5. Warm tortillas and fill with chicken tinga.
  6. Top with avocado, lime juice, cilantro, and cheese.
