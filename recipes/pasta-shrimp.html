Creamy Lemon Garlic Pasta

Home

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti or linguine
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt, pepper, red pepper flakes
  • Optional: spinach or arugula

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta and save ½ cup pasta water.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic (1-2 mins).
  3. Add cream, lemon zest/juice, Parmesan, and seasonings.
  4. Simmer until thickened slightly (3-5 mins).
  5. Add pasta + pasta water to the sauce. Toss well.
  6. Stir in greens if using. Serve with more Parmesan and pepper.
